How should the photo feel?

This sets the personality of the entire site. Pick the treatment that feels most like you.

CINEMATIC DARK

A — Cinematic Dark

Photo shot in near-darkness, strong rim light (green or blue). Like Jack Watkins. No smile needed — just presence. Feels: serious, confident, cinematic. Best if you have a photo with dramatic lighting.

DUOTONE GREEN

B — Duotone Green

Any photo, converted to a two-tone green/black treatment. Matches the #3fb950 accent perfectly. Feels: technical, on-brand, stylized. Works even with a casual photo — the treatment does the work.
