The Problem

A QLD seafood leader with a fishing fleet, 2 retail stores, wholesale ops and transport — running on an old Shopify store with no unified tech infrastructure. They needed someone to build it all from scratch.

The Hustle

Built their website before the interview. Walked in and said: "This website doesn't matter if you have no revenue from it." Got the job. First real production role — 24hrs/week as an international student.

What I Built

Eliminated Shopify (saving $20k+/yr). Built custom full-stack e-commerce with dynamic pricing, RBAC (super admin / admin / staff / wholesale), inventory system, delivery verification iOS app, and an AI-powered stock management pipeline — all as sole developer.

The Hard Parts

No production experience. Learned by talking to IBM, Amazon & CrowdStrike engineers on the bus. Navigated Australian privacy law, PCI DSS compliance, human resistance to change, and my own tendency to ship fast without documenting.

The Pipeline Built

E-commerce + Dynamic Pricing Inventory System Transport Booking App iOS Delivery Verification AI Stock Management RBAC + Wholesale Portal SEO / AEO / GEO

Medicrew

What if 8 AI doctors argued about your diagnosis?

Sister is a physiotherapist. Australian healthcare takes forever — especially in rural Gold Coast aged care areas. Built a multi-agent framework where 8 AI residents debate a diagnosis, a supervising agent synthesizes the outcome, and doctors get a structured report on their portal.

Applied to Alexa Lumina Health. Got rejected. Discovered Heidi was doing something similar — scraped it, iterated, redesigned. The rejection made it better.

Ambassador

The bus didn't stop. So I built the complaint system.

As an international student on Gold Coast public transport — the bus doesn't always stop. Nowhere to complain. Built a real-time geospatial complaint platform where AI classifies severity, maps issues by location, and council admins see a live command center.

Proof that AI belongs in everyday civic infrastructure — not just enterprise software.
